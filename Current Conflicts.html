Data Sources & Methodology

The estimates provided in this dashboard are aggregated from open-source intelligence, international monitoring organizations, defense budget reports, and geopolitical databases. For independent verification and real-time tracking, please consult the following primary organizations:

  • ACLED (Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project) - Primary source for fatality estimates and event tracking globally.
  • SIPRI (Stockholm International Peace Research Institute) - Global military expenditure, arms trade, and defense budget tracking.
  • UCDP (Uppsala Conflict Data Program) - Long-term historical conflict data and rigorous casualty metrics.

Methodology Note: Casualty and financial figures in active conflict zones are inherently difficult to verify and are subject to revision. The "Cost Per Casualty" and "% of GDP" metrics are quantitative derivations intended to highlight vast disparities in defense spending and economic impact; they are not intended to assign a monetary value to human life.
